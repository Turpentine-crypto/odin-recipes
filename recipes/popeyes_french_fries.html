Popeyes-style French Fries

Ultimate Result

Recreate the magic of Popeyes’ famous French fries right at home! Known for their perfectly seasoned, crispy exterior and fluffy interior, these fries are the ultimate side dish for any meal. With just the right amount of spice and crunch, this recipe mimics the flavors of the iconic fast-food favorite, delivering fries that are golden, flavorful, and absolutely irresistible. Perfect for a weekend treat, game day snack, or any time you're craving that signature Popeyes taste.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: Russet potatoes work best for that perfect balance of crispiness and fluffiness.
  • Seasoned Flour: A mix of all-purpose flour and cornstarch for the coating.
  • Seasoning Blend: Paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and salt.
  • Buttermilk: Adds a subtle tang and helps the seasoning adhere to the fries.
  • Oil for Frying: Vegetable or peanut oil for that signature crisp.

Preparation

  1. Cut the Potatoes: Slice the russet potatoes into thick, even strips. For best results, aim for a thickness similar to Popeyes’ fries. Rinse the potatoes in cold water to remove excess starch, then pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. Season the Flour: In a large bowl, combine the flour, cornstarch, and a generous amount of seasoning (paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne, salt, and pepper). This seasoned coating is key to getting that perfect crispy crust.
  3. Soak the Fries: Dunk the potato strips into buttermilk to add moisture and flavor, then dredge them in the seasoned flour mixture, making sure each fry is well coated.
  4. Fry the Potatoes: Heat oil in a large pot or deep fryer to 350°F (175°C). Fry the potatoes in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Cook until golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per batch. Remove the fries with a slotted spoon and drain them on paper towels.
  5. Season and Serve: While the fries are still hot, sprinkle them with an additional pinch of the seasoning blend. Serve immediately for that irresistible crunch.
